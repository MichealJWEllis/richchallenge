FAQ

Questions people will ask immediately.

Is this a charity?

No. This is not charity and gifts are not tax-deductible charitable donations.

Is this an investment?

No. There is no return, profit, equity, ownership, payout, or financial benefit promised to anyone who gifts.

Will gifting help me become the next featured person?

No. Gifting does not improve your odds. Applying for a future round is free.

Why $10 million?

Because the experiment is intentionally absurd but easy to understand: 10 million Rich Points equals $10 million gross before fees, taxes, holds, refunds, or other costs.

What happens after 3-6 months?

The round will be reviewed publicly. The review may include total gifted, estimated fees, net received, best-performing platforms, and whether another round makes sense.

Can minors participate?

No. This site is intended for adults only. Do not gift money you need.
